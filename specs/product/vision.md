# Product Vision — Mental Health Platform

## Mission
Create a safe, anonymous digital space where people can discuss mental health openly, connect with peers, and receive evidence-based health suggestions from verified human experts — enhanced by AI to scale expert reach without replacing human judgment.

## Target Users

| Persona | Description | Primary Need |
|---------|-------------|--------------|
| **Seeker** | Individual experiencing mental health challenges | Safe space to share, get peer support, find expert guidance |
| **Peer Supporter** | Community member with lived experience | Give back, help others, moderate discussions |
| **Expert** | Licensed therapist, psychologist, counselor | Extend reach, provide structured guidance at scale |
| **Admin** | Platform operator | Ensure safety, compliance, community health |

## Core Principles
1. **Anonymity First** — Users MUST be able to participate without revealing identity
2. **Safety is Non-Negotiable** — Crisis detection, content moderation, mandatory escalation paths
3. **Human Experts, AI-Assisted** — AI augments experts, never replaces them for clinical advice
4. **Evidence-Based** — All health suggestions must be grounded in clinical evidence
5. **Privacy by Design** — HIPAA-aware data handling, minimal data collection

## Phased Roadmap

### Phase 1: Anonymous Forum (MVP) — Current Focus
**Goal**: Safe, moderated forum for mental health discussions

Features:
- Anonymous user registration (email-verified but anonymous display)
- Forum categories (Anxiety, Depression, Relationships, Work Stress, etc.)
- Thread creation and reply system
- Basic keyword-based content moderation
- Crisis keyword detection with resource links
- User reporting and admin moderation dashboard

Success Metrics:
- 100 active monthly users within 3 months
- <2 hour response time for flagged content
- Zero undetected crisis posts

### Phase 2: AI-Enhanced Moderation
**Goal**: Scale content safety with AI assistance

Features:
- AI-powered content classification (toxic, crisis, supportive, neutral)
- Automated flagging with human review queue
- Sentiment analysis on threads
- Proactive wellness check prompts
- Community health dashboard

### Phase 3: Expert Integration
**Goal**: Bring verified mental health professionals onto the platform

Features:
- Expert onboarding with credential verification
- Expert profiles with specializations
- "Ask an Expert" threads (rate-limited, queue-managed)
- Expert availability scheduling
- Verified expert badge system

### Phase 4: AI-Assisted Health Suggestions
**Goal**: Scale expert knowledge with AI, under expert supervision

Features:
- AI draft suggestions based on expert-approved knowledge base
- Expert review and approval workflow for AI suggestions
- Personalized resource recommendations
- Structured self-help programs (CBT worksheets, mindfulness)
- Expert-curated content library

### Phase 5: Scale & Integrate
**Goal**: Mature platform with integrations

Features:
- Telehealth integration (video sessions)
- Insurance/payment processing
- Mobile apps (React Native)
- Multi-language support
- Research data partnerships (anonymized, consented)

## Out of Scope (Hard Boundaries)
- This platform does NOT provide clinical diagnosis
- This platform does NOT replace therapy
- AI NEVER gives clinical advice without expert review
- No social media features (likes, followers, vanity metrics)
- No gamification of mental health discussions
